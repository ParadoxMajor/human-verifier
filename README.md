# Human Verifier

ðŸ¤– Beep boop. Do you have trouble telling when a user is human? Do you not allow bots to post and comment on your subreddit? Install this app to help!

Just select the 'Verify Human' mod action from a post or comment, and flag them for verification. It notifies them via modmail that they've been requested to confirm they are human and explains how to complete it. Until they confirm, their posts and comments are automatically removed (by default, can be adjusted for reporting or doing nothing in settings). If they take too long to confirm, they can be considered automatically failed, as well as other options in the app settings.

Modmail verification requests are auto-archived for your peace of mind (unless they reply, of course).
